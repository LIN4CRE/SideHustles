## 4. The launch plan (first 7 days)

Listing the products is 20% of the job. Traffic is the other 80%. Here's a realistic, £0-budget first week.

**Day 1 — Go live.** Publish the bundle + all 8 products on Gumroad. Set the `LAUNCH40` code (40% off, 72h). Screenshot your store.

**Day 2 — Reddit (value-first).** Post genuine value in r/juststart, r/sidehustle, r/Entrepreneur, r/passive_income — share ONE actual tip from a product (e.g. 3 of the cold-email rules), then mention the full pack in a comment or your profile, not the post body. Never hard-sell; Reddit punishes it.

**Day 3 — X/Twitter + LinkedIn.** Post a thread using your own hooks from Product 06 ("I built 8 digital products in a weekend — here's the exact playbook"). Give real value, link the bundle at the end.

**Day 4 — Etsy cross-list.** Put the wallpaper pack and the templates on Etsy for evergreen search traffic. Use the Etsy-SEO titles above.

**Day 5 — Pinterest (for wallpapers).** Pin your best 5 wallpapers with keyword-rich descriptions linking to the Etsy/Gumroad listing. Pinterest is a search engine and OLED wallpapers get saved and clicked for months.

**Day 6 — TikTok/Reels.** Make 2–3 short videos using your own hooks: "POV: you found the side-hustle bundle that does the work for you." Show the products on screen. Link in bio.

**Day 7 — Communities & DMs.** Share in relevant Discord/Facebook groups where self-promo is allowed. Message anyone who engaged during the week.

**Ongoing:** repost winners, add a new product monthly, collect and display every review, and build an email list (Gumroad captures buyer emails automatically) so your second launch sells to people who already trust you.

**The honest truth about the numbers:** digital products are a volume-and-traffic game. Expect the first few sales to come from effort, not luck. The bundle at £14.99 needs ~7 sales to clear £100 after fees. The real leverage is that once it's built (done — it's built), every future sale is near-100% margin and costs you nothing. Keep driving traffic and it compounds.
