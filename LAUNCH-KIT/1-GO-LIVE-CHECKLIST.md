# Go-Live Checklist — From Files to Taking Payments (~15 minutes)

**Everything is built. This is the only part that must be you** — because taking money requires *your* identity and *your* bank/PayPal on a payout account. No tool can create that for you, and I won't pretend a sale happened that didn't. Do these steps and you are genuinely live and able to receive money.

---

## Step 1 — Create your store (5 min)

Pick one (you can add the others later):

- **Gumroad** (recommended for speed) → gumroad.com → Start selling → sign up.
- **Payhip** (recommended if you want UK/EU VAT handled for you) → payhip.com → sign up.

Both are free to start and take no upfront money.

## Step 2 — Connect your payout (3 min)

- **Gumroad:** Settings → Payments → connect your bank details (UK bank supported) or PayPal.
- **Payhip:** Settings → connect Stripe and/or PayPal.

☐ This is the step only you can do. Once done, money from sales lands in your account automatically.

## Step 3 — Create the products (5 min)

Upload from the `LAUNCH-KIT` files:

1. **Create the Mega Bundle first** (your best seller):
   - New product → Digital product.
   - Upload `SideHustle-Starter-Mega-Bundle.zip`.
   - Title, description, price (£14.99) → copy straight from `STOREFRONT-KIT.md`.
   - Cover image: use `thumbs/` or any product cover.
2. **Then add the 8 individual products** (upload each PDF from `individual-products/`, paste its listing from the Storefront Kit). The wallpaper product: upload `OLED-Wallpaper-Pack.zip` instead of the bare PDF so buyers get the images too.

☐ Add the `LAUNCH40` discount code (40% off, expires in 72h) to create launch urgency.

## Step 4 — Publish & grab your links (1 min)

- Hit Publish on each. Copy the bundle's checkout URL.

## Step 5 — Put up the landing page (optional, 5 min)

- Open `index.html`, find the line near the bottom: `const BUY_URL = "REPLACE_WITH_YOUR_STORE_LINK";`
- Paste your bundle checkout URL between the quotes, save.
- Deploy free: drag the file onto **app.netlify.com/drop**, or push to **Cloudflare Pages** / **Vercel**. You'll get a public link in seconds.

## Step 6 — Drive traffic (ongoing — this is where sales come from)

Follow the **7-day launch plan** in `STOREFRONT-KIT.md`. Listing is 20% of the work; putting it in front of people every day is the other 80%. Start with the Day-1 Reddit and X posts today.
